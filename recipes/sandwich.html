Ham Sandwich

Description

This recipe is a ham sandwich made on a baguette to make it tasty. It is recommended to have a panini press to make this recipe but grilling it on the pan is also an option. Please enjoy a quick, easy to make and delicious sandwich.

Ingredients

  • Baguette
  • Pesto
  • Provolone Cheese
  • Ham
  • Lemon
  • Cherry Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Preheat panini press to 400 degrees or turn on burner under pan if that is not an option
  2. Cut open bageutte
  3. Spread pesto on bageutte
  4. Put three to five slices of ham on bageutte
  5. Put 2 slices of cheese on bageutte
  6. Slice cherry tomatoes and put them on bageutte
  7. Put sandwich on the panini press or pan until golden brown
  8. Squeeze lemon on sandwich
