Pasta

Description

This recipe is a general one on how to make tasty pasta.

Ingredients

  • Pasta of choice
  • Marinara sauce
  • Boiling water
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Set water to boil in a pot on the stove
  2. Once boiling, salt the water and then add the pasta
  3. Wait for 5-10 minutes or until soft and pastalike, stirring occasionally
  4. Pour pasta into a collander
  5. Put pasta back into the pot
  6. Pour marinara sauce in and stir
  7. Enjoy!
